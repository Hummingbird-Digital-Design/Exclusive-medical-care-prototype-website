About Us

EMC Ltd was set-up by a group experienced healthcare professionals who identified the lack of options available to those companies and individuals who need and demand the highest standard of medical care. Exclusive Medical Care Ltd (EMC Ltd) is a UK registered company which specialises in providing first class private medical care to our overseas clients. Through collaboration with our national and international partners, we are able to facilitate all medical needs to the highest of standards.

Exclusive Medical Care Ltd also offers its clients the opportunity to expand on their medical expertise by offering to them international medical education, medical training and human resource development within the medical sector. We are able to offer our expertise through our extensive range of international partners, which ensures the most effective delivery. EMC Ltd has established relationships with both governmental and public sector educational facilities and medical institutions across the Middle East and North Africa.
